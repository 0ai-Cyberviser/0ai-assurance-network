# Contributing

External contributions are not automatically accepted.

By submitting a patch, proposal, design, or issue contribution to this
repository, you confirm that:

- you have the right to submit the material
- the submission does not violate third-party rights
- you understand the project is owner-controlled and may reject, rewrite, or
  decline to merge your submission

No contribution changes ownership of the project. Accepted submissions may be
integrated under the repository's governing license and control model.

Security-sensitive matters should not be opened as public issues. Use
`SECURITY.md` instead.
