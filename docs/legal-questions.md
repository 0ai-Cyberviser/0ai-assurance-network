# Legal Questions Tracker

## Open Questions

1. What token activities, if any, create registration or disclosure obligations
   in the primary launch jurisdictions?
2. Which entities in the stack, if any, are acting as administrators,
   exchangers, or money transmitters?
3. What sanctions and AML controls are mandatory for the intended service model?
4. What treasury and governance disclosures are required before any public
   network participation?
5. Which incident and consumer-protection disclosures apply if safety
   attestations are marketed as an enterprise assurance product?

## Required Outputs

- external counsel memo
- jurisdiction matrix
- AML and sanctions policy
- disclosure checklist
- contribution and governance liability review
